Information about the city

Itauguá is a city located in the Central Department, one of Paraguay's administrative regions. It is situated approximately 27 kilometers east of Asunción, the capital of the country. Itauguá is renowned for its rich cultural and artisanal tradition, particularly in the crafting of musical instruments and ñandutí, a traditional Paraguayan lace.

The city holds historical and cultural significance and has been the backdrop for significant events in Paraguay's history. In addition to its cultural heritage, Itauguá boasts natural attractions in its surroundings, making it an interesting destination for visitors.
